Service 02

Sewer Scope Inspections

A sewer scope inspection uses a waterproof camera system to inspect the underground main sewer line from the home to the municipal connection or septic system. Hidden problems beneath the ground can be identified before they become catastrophic and extremely costly repairs.

The camera is introduced through a cleanout access point and records a real-time video of the pipe's interior, revealing any issues present.

  • Root intrusion detection
  • Cracks and pipe separation
  • Blockages and buildup
  • Pipe corrosion and deterioration
  • Bellied or sagging sections
  • Offset joints
⚠ Sewer line repairs can cost tens of thousands of dollars. A scope inspection is a small investment that can prevent a massive surprise expense.

Service 03

Radon Testing

Radon is an invisible, odorless, radioactive gas that forms naturally from uranium decay in soil and rock. It can seep into homes through foundation cracks, openings around pipes, and construction joints — accumulating to dangerous concentrations without any visible sign.

We use professional charcoal packet radon testing to detect radon levels inside the home. The EPA recommends mitigation if indoor radon levels are at or above 4 pCi/L.

  • Charcoal packet testing method
  • Placed in lowest livable area of home
  • Lab-analyzed results
  • EPA action level guidance included
  • Fast turnaround on results
